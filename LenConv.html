For Km to Mm
(International System of Units)




How it works :
- Example : Km to M (Descending) : Km to M is 3 blocks down , so :

Km = M x 10 x 10 x 10
OR
M x 1000

- Example : M to Km (Ascending) : M to Km is 3 blocks up , so :

M = Km / 10 / 10 / 10
OR
Km / 1000


For Scientific Notation rules , you can use "e" , Example :

2 x 10^2 or 2x10^-2
Just input 2e2 or 2e-2
"e = 10^n"
2e2 = 2 x 10^2 and 2e-2 = 2 x 10^-2
